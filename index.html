Cutting-Edge Features, Affordable price


The Scout Infinity was developed for the next generation of mobile communication. It can send texts and utilize data anywhere with the global LoRaWAN network and interface with user-created apps, for a price less than $100. The Infinity connects to both iPhone and Android devices using Bluetooth Low Energy and charges wirelessly with NFC. While designing the Infinity, we endeavored to create a sleek and easy to use piece of hardware with an intuitive interface to match.

Built on the Most Trusted IoT Network


Since 2015, TheThingsNetwork has provided LoRaWAN service all over the world. With over 12,000 gateways across 150 countries and partners like Amazon and Cisco, TheThingsNetwork has become the standard for LoRaWAN networks. We plan to utilize this reliability and security to bring you the best experience possible.

Crowdsourced Communications


Scout believes in a truly open network for mobile communications, instead of a centralized and commercialized approach. That's why we have dropped all service related fees— no monthly bill , no subscription services, and certainly no hidden fees.
